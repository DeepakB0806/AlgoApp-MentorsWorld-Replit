Currently, in server/te-kotak-neo-v3.ts, the delay between placing/closing legs is hardcoded to 100ms in the Entry, Exit, and Reversal loops. Furthermore, the squareOffPlan loop is completely missing a delay, meaning it fires closing orders instantly, which can trigger broker rate limits or Risk Management (RMS) margin rejections if the broker hasn't processed the previous leg yet.
We need to replace the hardcoded 100ms with a dynamic fetch from the database (order_execution_delay_ms) and inject this delay into the squareOffPlan loop.
